## Book Structure

The book has three parts:
- **Book One**: Gematria (number-letter mysticism)
- **Book Two**: Liber 777 (the correspondence tables) — pp. 1-146
- **Book Three**: Sepher Sephiroth (dictionary of sacred numbers)

Book Two contains 6 main Tables plus Explanations:
- **Table I** (pp. 2-16): Core correspondences — 34 rows (0-32 + 32bis + 31bis) x ~21 columns
- **Table II** (pp. 17-18): Archangels, elements, rivers, senses, demon kings, court cards
- **Table III** (p. 19): Planetary spirits, intelligences, Olympic spirits, hexagram
- **Table IV** (pp. 20-27): Extensive — Briah palaces/angels/names, Yetzirah angelic functions, Assiah heavens/saints/angels/archangels, soul parts, chakras, Qliphoth, hells, alchemy, Masonic, grades, magical images, Plagues of Egypt, Arab heavens
- **Table V** (pp. 27-33): Zodiacal (rows 15-29 only) — Tarot minor suits, decan images, Goetia demons, perfumes, angels, lunar mansions, Egyptian decans
- **Table VI** (pp. 35-36): Tarot trump numbers, geomantic intelligences, Yetziratic attributions, Hebrew numerical values, legendary beings, human body parts
- **Explanations of Attributions** (pp. 45-124): Crowley's notes on each column

## Lightning Flash / Flaming Sword Sequence (Column IX)

The Lightning Flash traces the descent of divine energy through the 10 Sephiroth:

**Kether (1) -> Chokmah (2) -> Binah (3) -> Chesed (4) -> Geburah (5) -> Tiphareth (6) -> Netzach (7) -> Hod (8) -> Yesod (9) -> Malkuth (10)**

From the 777 notes (Col IX, p. 5): "The Flaming Sword follows the downward course of the Sephiroth, and is the course of the paths or letters upward, its head being in Kether and its hilt in Malkuth."

The **Serpent of Wisdom** ascends the reverse path, following the 22 connecting paths from Malkuth back to Kether.

## Cultural Context

Crowley's 777 is not original scholarship but a systematic compilation of the Golden Dawn's received tradition, drawing on:
- **Sefer Yetzirah** (Hebrew letter classification, 3+7+12 structure)
- **Zohar** (Sephirotic emanation, color symbolism)
- **Golden Dawn ritual papers** (Tarot attributions, color scales)
- **Kircher** (Tree of Life path layout)
- **Various mythological and religious sources** (Egyptian, Greek, Roman, Hindu, Buddhist, Norse)

The Four Color Scales represent the most systematic attempt to assign colors to abstract metaphysical concepts. They progress from pure archetypal colors (King/Atziluth) through increasingly material/mixed colors (Empress/Assiah), mirroring the Kabbalistic doctrine of progressive materialization of divine light.

The 777 system creates a universal correspondence matrix where any symbol from any tradition can be cross-referenced with any other through their shared Key Scale number. This is the structural basis for Track 19 (Unified Cycles).
