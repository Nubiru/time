### Tsaddi/He Swap (*)

Crowley's key innovation: **"Tzaddi is not the Star"** (Liber AL I:57).

Traditional attribution: Star=He(Aquarius), Emperor=Tzaddi(Aries)
Crowley swap: **Star=He'(Aquarius)**, **Emperor=Tzaddi(Aries)**

The LETTERS swap but the ZODIACAL signs stay with their traditional cards. This creates zodiacal symmetry on the Tree:
- Emperor (Aries) moves from path 15 (Chokmah→Tiphareth) to path 28 (Netzach→Yesod)
- Star (Aquarius) moves from path 28 to path 15

Similarly, VIII and XI swap numbers but keep their zodiacal signs:
- VIII = Adjustment (Libra) — traditionally XI Justice
- XI = Lust (Leo) — traditionally VIII Strength

### Three Mother Letters
- Aleph (Air) = The Fool — above/beyond the Tree
- Mem (Water) = The Hanged Man — surrender, reversal
- Shin (Fire) = The Aeon — transformation, new age

### Seven Double Letters (Planets)
- Beth (Mercury) = The Magus
- Gimel (Moon) = The Priestess
- Daleth (Venus) = The Empress
- Kaph (Jupiter) = Fortune
- Peh (Mars) = The Tower

### Twelve Simple Letters (Zodiac)
Aries=Emperor, Taurus=Hierophant, Gemini=Lovers, Cancer=Chariot,
Leo=Lust, Virgo=Hermit, Libra=Adjustment, Scorpio=Death,

## Q4: Time-Cycle Associations

### The Three Aeons (Precessional Time-Cycles)

Crowley's primary time-cycle framework, tied to precession of the equinoxes (~2000 years per Aeon):

| Aeon | Deity | Character | Approximate Period | Formula |
|------|-------|-----------|-------------------|---------|
| Isis | Great Mother | Matriarchal, nature worship | Ancient past | Female principle |
| Osiris | Dying God | Patriarchal, sacrifice, redemption | ~0 CE – 1904 CE | Male principle |
| Horus | Crowned & Conquering Child | Individualist, "every man and woman is a star" | 1904 CE – ~3900 CE | Child (union of opposites) |

**Key date**: 1904 CE — Beginning of the Aeon of Horus. "This was accomplished in the year of the vulgar era 1904, when the fiery god Horus took the place of the airy god Osiris in the East as Hierophant."

**Next Aeon**: "presumably in about 2,000 years — 'the fall of the Great Equinox; when Hrumachis shall arise and the double-wanded one assume my throne and place'."

### Card-Specific Time Associations

| Card | Association | Season/Time |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| VII Chariot | Cancer | Summer Solstice ("Cancer is the sign of the Summer Solstice") |
| XV Devil | Capricorn | Winter Solstice ("the Sun turns northwards on entering this sign") |
| XVIII Moon | Pisces | End of winter ("the last stage of winter... Gateway of Resurrection") |
| XIX Sun | Sun/zodiac | All seasons ("signs of the Zodiac in their normal position, Aries rising in the East") |
| XX Aeon | Fire/Shin | 1904 CE, ~2000-year precessional periods |
| V Hierophant | Taurus | "Moves at intervals of 2,000 years" (precessional) |
| XI Lust | Leo | Succession of Aeons (Leo = Kerubic fire sign, power of the New Aeon) |

### Aeon Cards in the Thoth Deck
- **The Aeon (XX)** replaces the old "Last Judgment" — directly encodes the 1904 transition
- **The Hierophant (V)** — "age-old Aeon" formula, 2000-year cycles
- **Lust (XI)** — "Babalon riding the Beast" = the New Aeon's formula of courage
- **The Star (XVII)** — Nuith, "Our Lady of the Stars" = the cosmic matrix within which Aeons unfold

### The Three Vowel-Consonants (IAO Formula)
Aleph (Fool, 0), Yod (Hermit, IX), Ayin (Devil, XV) — "these three letters form the sacred name of God, I A O. These three Atu, IX, 0, and XV, thus offer a threefold explanation of the male creative energy."

## Q5: YHVH / Tetragrammaton / Four Worlds Formula

### The Core Formula

```
YHVH = Yod-He-Vav-He
     = Father-Mother-Son-Daughter
     = Fire-Water-Air-Earth
     = Knight-Queen-Prince-Princess
     = Wands-Cups-Swords-Disks
     = Atziluth-Briah-Yetzirah-Assiah
```

### Parts of the Soul (mapped to Sephirotic ranges)

| Sephirah(s) | Part of Soul | YHVH Level | World |
|-------------|-------------|------------|-------|
| 1 (Kether) | Jechidah (Divine Self) | Topmost point of Yod | Above all |
| 2 (Chokmah) | Chiah (Creative Will) | Yod = Father | Atziluth |
| 3 (Binah) | Neschamah (Intuition) | He = Mother | Briah |
| 4-9 (Chesed→Yesod) | Ruach (Intellect) | Vav = Son | Yetzirah |
| 10 (Malkuth) | Nephesch (Animal Soul) | He final = Daughter | Assiah |

### The Revolving Formula
"No sooner has the Princess made her appearance than the Prince wins her in marriage, and she is set upon the throne of her Mother. She thus awakens the Eld of the original old King; who thereupon becomes a young Knight, and so renews the cycle."

This is the **perpetual cycle**: Daughter→throne of Mother→awakens Father→becomes Knight→cycle repeats. The Tetragrammaton is not static but a revolving, self-renewing process.

### The Pentagrammaton (YHVH + Shin = YHShVH)
"The letter Shin, which is the letter of Fire, has to do double duty by representing Spirit as well." Shin inserted into YHVH produces YHShVH (Yeheshua/Jesus), adding the 5th element of Spirit. This is represented by the Aeon card (XX).

## Cultural Context

The Book of Thoth represents Crowley's synthesis of the Western magical tradition's understanding of time and cycles. The Tarot is presented not as a fortune-telling device but as a **complete symbolic model of the universe** — every card maps to a Hebrew letter, a path on the Tree of Life, a zodiacal or planetary force, and a position in the YHVH cycle of creation.

The Three Aeons framework (Isis→Osiris→Horus) maps precessional cycles to cultural evolution, making it directly relevant to the Time project's cross-system convergence tracking. The ~2000-year Aeon period aligns with the precessional "Great Month" of ~2160 years.

The court card → I Ching hexagram cross-references are unique to Crowley and provide a direct bridge between Western (Tarot) and Eastern (Yi King) divination systems — exactly the kind of structural unity the Time project maps.
