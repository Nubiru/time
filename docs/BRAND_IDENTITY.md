## Name

**TIME** — all capitals. A declaration, not a description.

## Philosophy

One number governs everything: **phi = 1.618...**

The golden ratio appears in sunflower seed arrangements, galaxy arm curvature, DNA double helix proportions, the Parthenon, the Great Pyramid, and musical harmonic intervals.

TIME proves this visually. Every font size, spacing value, animation timing, opacity level, and color relationship derives from phi. The UI IS the proof.

---

## Brand Colors

Three colors define TIME's identity. They are **theme-invariant** — identical in Cosmos (dark) and Dawn (light) themes.

| Name | Float RGB | Hex | Symbolism |
|------|-----------|-----|-----------|
| **Solar Gold** | (1.0, 0.85, 0.55) | #FFD98C | The Sun — source of all earthly time |
| **Celestial Teal** | (0.2, 0.75, 0.8) | #33BFCC | The cosmic bridge between knowledge systems |
| **Space Black** | (0.024, 0.027, 0.036) | #060709 | The canvas — deep blue-black, alive, not dead |

### Usage Rules

- **Solar Gold**: Primary accent, interactive highlights, brand mark center, the Sun
- **Celestial Teal**: Secondary accent, knowledge system bridges, outer spiral, cosmic depth
- **Space Black**: Primary background (Cosmos theme), shadows, negative space. Never pure #000000.

### Relationship to phi

Gold and Teal are separated by approximately the golden angle (137.5 degrees) on the hue wheel. Gold at ~45 degrees, Teal at ~185 degrees. Delta = 140 degrees, close to the golden angle. This is intentional — even the brand colors follow phi.

## Logo: The Phi Spiral

A golden spiral (logarithmic spiral with growth factor phi) rendered as a luminous arc on Space Black.

### Mathematical Construction

```
r(theta) = a * phi^(2 * theta / PI)
```

Each quarter-turn of the spiral grows by exactly phi. The spiral completes ~2.5 revolutions from center to edge.

### Visual Properties

- **Line width**: Tapers outward — phi^-0.35 per revolution. Thicker at center (the Sun), thinner at edge (the cosmos).
- **Color gradient**: Solar Gold at center, transitioning smoothly to Celestial Teal at the outer edge.
- **Glow**: Soft bloom aura around the spiral line, sigma proportional to line width.
- **Center point**: Bright warm glow (the Sun — the origin of time).
- **Background**: Space Black with sparse subtle star dust.

### Why the golden spiral

- phi IS the project's mathematical soul — every visual value derives from it
- Spirals appear in galaxies (astronomy), nautilus shells (nature), orbital mechanics
- The spiral moving outward = time flowing, knowledge expanding concentrically
- It is a single curve that encodes the entire project philosophy
- It is procedurally generated — no image assets. Consistent with "every pixel earned"

### Implementation

The logo is a GLSL fragment shader: `shaders/logo.frag`. The shader IS the logo. No image file exists. The code is the artwork.

Preview: `web/logo-preview.html` (open via `make serve` at localhost:8080/logo-preview.html)

## Loading Screen Animation

The spiral draws itself — starting from the center point (the Sun, the origin), curling outward through 2.5 revolutions. Phi-timed:

| Phase | Duration | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Draw | 2.618s (phi^2) | Spiral curls from center outward, eased deceleration |
| Tip glow | During draw | Bright point at the leading edge of the drawing |
| Breathing | After draw | Subtle pulse at phi^-1 Hz (0.618 Hz, period = 1.618s) |
| Wordmark | After draw | "TIME" fades in over 0.618s (deferred until SDF fonts exist) |

## Color System Integration

The brand colors live in `src/ui/theme.h` as fields of `theme_t`:
- `brand_primary` = Solar Gold
- `brand_secondary` = Celestial Teal
- `bg_space` = Space Black

System-specific palettes (`src/render/color_theory.h`) layer on top. When a knowledge system is focused, its personality color becomes the accent via `theme_system_accent()`. The brand colors are the neutral foundation underneath.
